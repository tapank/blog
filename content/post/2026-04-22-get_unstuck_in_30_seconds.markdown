Stanford lecturer and entrepreneur Graham Weaver [said this about getting
unstuck](https://www.youtube.com/shorts/Jw6ZxrT9k10):

If you are stuck in a rut, answer these questions.

1. What am I avoiding?
2. Where do I start?
3. How do I win today?
4. What are the habits that are interfering with where I want to go?
